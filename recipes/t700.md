# t700: Temperature at 700hPa

Mixer: **default**

## Inputs

* **t700**:
    * Model **cosmo**:
        * **Arkimet matcher**: `product:GRIB1,,2,11;level:GRIB1,100,700`
        * **grib_filter matcher**: `shortName is "t" and indicatorOfTypeOfLevel == 100 and level == 700`
    * Model **ifs**:
        * **Arkimet matcher**: `product:GRIB1,,2,11;level:GRIB1,100,700`
        * **grib_filter matcher**: `shortName is "t" and indicatorOfTypeOfLevel == 100 and level == 700`
